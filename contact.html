Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I be matched with a financial advisor?

Most clients are matched with qualified financial advisors within 24-48 hours of completing our onboarding process.

Is there a cost to use your matching service?

No, our matching service is completely free for investors. You only pay advisory fees directly to the advisor you choose to work with.

What if I'm not satisfied with my matched advisor?

We offer unlimited re-matching until you find the right advisor for your needs. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you work with advisors nationwide?

Yes, we have a network of certified financial advisors across all 50 states, offering both in-person and virtual consultation options.

How do you verify advisor credentials?

All advisors in our network undergo rigorous background checks, credential verification, and ongoing compliance monitoring.

Can I work with multiple advisors?

Yes, you can interview multiple advisors before making your decision. We encourage speaking with several to find the best fit.
