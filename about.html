Our Story

Founded in 2020, FinanceAdvisor was born from a simple observation: finding the right financial advisor shouldn't be complicated, time-consuming, or expensive. Too many people were struggling to navigate the complex world of financial planning without proper guidance.

Our founders, experienced financial professionals and technology experts, recognized that artificial intelligence and modern technology could revolutionize how people connect with financial advisors. Today, we've helped thousands of investors find their perfect financial advisor match.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality financial advice, regardless of their wealth level or investment experience. Our platform combines the personal touch of human expertise with the efficiency of AI-powered matching.
